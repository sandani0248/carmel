About Incor

INCOR is constantly breaking conventions to redefine a better lifestyle every day. The Group believes in building homes that can help you live a life more enriching and meaningful. Homes that are lifemarks, not landmarks.

The Group started its journey with PBEL City Hyderabad which is now one of the most sought-after housing society in the city with more than 1500 families residing there. The first phase of PBEL City Chennai was launched to introduce the concept of community living. Presently, around 400 families have already made it their home, making it one of the most vibrant residential projects in the city. The other projects of INCOR - ONE CITY and Divino Villas in Hyderabad are in advanced stages of development.

With Carmel Heights, INCOR is all set to redefine the real estate ecosystem in Bangalore.
